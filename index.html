Let's play ping pong!

Let's play a number game! It's called ping pong, but it's a bit different than the table game. It's super simple, just enter a number! Then the program will give you alllll numbers leading up to that number and it will:

  • Replace all numbers divisible by 3 with "ping"
  • Replace all numbers divisible by 5 with "pong"
  • Replace all numbers divisible by 15 with "ping-pong"

Have fun!
